Welcome

The IEEE LaunchPad Kerala Newsletter is a lively space that connects skilled professionals with innovative businesses in the ever-changing realms of technology and engineering. This platform is a result of the collaborative efforts of the IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. Our mission is clear: to create valuable relationships, promote progress, and ignite innovation within Kerala's thriving tech-focused workforce.
